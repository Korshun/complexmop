Complex Doom is made by Daedalus.
Master of Puppets is made by Silva.

Complex MOP is an addon for Complex Doom that brings Master of Puppets game mode to Complex Doom.

Only the code necessary to make possessed monsters behave like Complex Doom ones was taken from Complex Doom.

ACS scripts are original and are not taken from Master of Puppets, except some hud messages and victory songs.

Complex MOP is made by Korshun, Monsterovich and DJ Skaarj.
